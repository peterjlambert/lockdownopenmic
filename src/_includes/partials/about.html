What's this now?

Musicians, poets, comics, plate-spinners. Got an urge to perform in this time of social isolation? We’re here for you. The pubs and clubs are closed and your favourite local open-mic night is sadly on haitus. Join our virtual open-mic and give us a bit of entertainment.

It’ll be friendly, welcoming and the drinks will be cheap (providing you’ve got some in). From seasoned performers to first-timers - come and give it a go.

Is this for me?

I dunno. Do you like to play an instrument, sing, write poetry or make people laugh? Got an urge to perform for others but can’t because of the lockdown? Want to share an experience with some like-minded folk? Then yes, this could be for you. Why not give it a try?
