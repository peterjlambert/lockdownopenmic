How do I join in?

If you'd like to join the show, just send me an email (pete@lockdownopenmic.club) and I'll send you the joining link.

We use Zoom for the Lockdown Open Mic - an app available for every platform, phone, tablet, desktop, smart fridge, lawnmower etc. (Click here for tips on using Zoom for playing music and make sure you enable 'Original Sound)

Drop me an email to let me know you're interested and I'll share the joining information. Then you just need to turn up any time from 9pm (UK time), turn on your camera, mute your mic, then if you want to perform let me know in the Zoom text chat and I'll put you on the list - just like a real open mic!

We'll do one song per performer at a time and move around the group to keep things flowing.
